About Us

Nepal is a beautiful country with an area of 147,181 square kilometer.It lies between two Asian giants: China and India.This rectangular country is 885 km in east-west andaverage of 160 km in North-South.It may be small in size but it is a country of vast geological and cultural diversity.It has an extreme altitude variation on earth from lowland of terai, 65 meters abobe sea level to the highest peak on the Mt.Everest at 8848 meters.Its is also known as vertical world-being steepest country and third pole-having many tallest peaks.The Himalayan range running from Pakistan through India,Nepal and Bhutanis the longest Himalayan range on earth and one third of that range lies in Nepal comprising tallest peak in the world. Nepal has world’s 8 tallest mountains out of the top 10. Beside that it has 1300 peaks above 6000 meters.Sherpas are the native inhabitants of this himalayan region and are world renowned for their incredible climbing skills.

Once we get down the mountains range the green hills there to welcome us. The major inhabitants are bahun,chettri,newar the indo- aryan race. The hilly village of Gorkha is the birth place of world’s deadliest fighters Gorkha who serve in the Indian and British Army regiments and are well known for their bravery. In Nepal, there are more than 100 ethinic groups and sub-groups who speak over 93 language and dialect in here.Nepal offers an astoniozing diversity of sightseeing attractions and adventure oppertunities found nowhere else on earth.And one can join in the numerous annual festivals that are celebrated throughout the year in tradidional style highlighting enduring customs and beliefs. The low land Terai, is the food bank of country and culturally rich place. Most of the people here are Maithalis, Tharus and endure their own culture and way of life. The low land is a perfect habitat for numerous Flora and Fuanas. In fact because of the variance is climate,whole country is a habitat of various plants and animals including more than 900 species of birds.

Kathmandu valley is made of Three cities: Kathmandu,Lalitpur and Bhaktapur. It is a melting pot of rich history and modernization. There is a saying that “there is more temples than houses” in these three cities and no doubt it is but truth. The amazing alliance of Hinduism and Buddhism can be seen in every nooks and corners in here. The arts and artifacts as old as 5th-6th century are well preserved in this valley and it is hard to believe how different the capital city is from others parts of the country and yet every morning from the windows and roof of every houses one would see the view of breath taking Himalayas in here.

All in All Nepal is a world with in a world. The diversity is so vast that one can be shocked with what it has to offer in outer world from the foothills of Mt.Everest to wheat field of Terai , the peoples, language, culture, religion, food everythings is different. The only things that is common here is the HEART of a Nepalese peoples which is warm and welcoming to treat guests as “GODS” .
